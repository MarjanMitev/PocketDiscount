# User Personas - PocketDiscount

## 1. The "Efficient Planner" (Lisa)

* **Role:** Working mom of two.
* **Goal:** Feed the family healthy meals on a budget without spending 3 hours shopping.
* **Pain Point:** Hates visiting 3 different websites to check flyers (folders).
* **App Usage:** Uses the **Basket Optimizer**. Typos in "Milk, Bread, Cheese" -> App tells her "Go to Lidl first, then swing by Jumbo on the way home."

## 2. The "Deals Hunter" (Mark)

* **Role:** Student / Young Professional.
* **Goal:** Maximum savings, willing to travel.
* **Pain Point:** Needs to know *exactly* where the cheapest beer/frozen pizza is.
* **App Usage:** Uses the **Map View**. Filters for "Beer > 50% discount" and drives to the specific Dirk location showing the deal.

## 3. The "Inflation Watcher" (Sarah)

* **Role:** Budget-conscious retiree.
* **Goal:** Track buying habits and ensure she isn't overspending.
* **App Usage:** Uses the **Receipt Scanner**. Scans every receipt to build a history. Checks the "Future Bonus" prediction to see if she should wait until next week to buy coffee.
